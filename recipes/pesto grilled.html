recipe

odine recipe

desciption

this chicken avocado salad is unique in that its pesto dressing also works as a marinade.salads will never be boring with this recipe

ingridients

  • salads;4 cups romaine or lettuce leaves washed and dried
  • 1 large cucumber diced
  • 1 cup grape or cherry diced
  • tomatoes and red onion sliced
  • 1 avacado sliced
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta
  • dressing;1/3 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoon basil pesto[homemade or store bought]
  • 2 tablespoon lemon juice[adjust to your taste]
  • 1-2 tablespoon water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoon garlic minced
  • 4[1 pound or 500g]skinless,bornless chicken thigh fillets or chicken breasts

steps

  1. whisk together all of the dressing ingredients in a large jar,pour out half of the dressing into a large shallow dish and refrain the remaining untouched marinade to use as a dressing later
  2. add the chicken to the marinade in the bowl ;marinade chicken for 15-30min or 2hrs in the refridgerator if time allows,while waiting for the chicken prepare all the salades ingredients and mix in a large salad bowl
  3. once this is ready heat 1tablespoon of oil in a gril pan or a grill plate over medium high heat,grill chicken on both sides untill browned and completely cooked through.
  4. allow the chicken to rest for 5min and arrange over salades with remaining untouched dressing,serve with lemon wedges,extra basil pesto and chopped basil or parsley

    5. enjoy!!!!!!!!!
