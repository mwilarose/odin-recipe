recipe

odine recipe

description

this vegetable soup is easy to make amd taste incredible.there are no fancy ingridients and everyting needed is affordable,its health,comforting and completely vegetarian

ingredients

  • 3 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 cups chopped onion [1 medium]
  • 1 1/2 cups chopped carrots[3-4 carrots]
  • 1 1/2 cups chopped celery[2-3 stalks]
  • 2 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 4 teaspoon minced garlic[4 cloves]
  • 3/4 teaspoon grounded funnel seed
  • 1/2 teaspoon grounded blackpaper
  • 1/2 teaspoon fine seasalt,plus more to taste
  • 1/8-1/4 teaspoon crushed red powder flakes[optional for heat]
  • 1[15-ounce]can diced tomatoes with their liquid
  • 6 cups stock or broth,see our ultra-satisfying veggie broth or homemade chicken stock
  • 2 cups chopped potatoes[2 medium]
  • 3-4 heaped cups chopped or shredded cabbage
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 1/2 teaspoon apple cider vinegar or fresh lemon juice,optional

steps

  1. heat the oil in a stockpot or duch over medium heat.add the onion,carrots,celery and tomato paste.cook,stirring often untill the vegetables have soften and the onion are translucent;8-10min
  2. add the garlic,fennel,black pepper,1/2 teaspoon of salt and the red pepper flakes.cook,while stirring for 1min
  3. pour in the canned tomatoes and their juice as well as the stock/broth
  4. add the potatoes,cabbage and bay leaves.raise the heat to medium high and bring the soup to a boil.partially cover the pot with a lid and then reduce the heat to maintain a low simmer.
  5. simmer for 20min or untill the potatoes or other vegetables are tender.add the frozen peas and cook for five more minutes
  6. remove the pot from the heat and remove the bay leaves,stir in the cider vinegar[or lemon juice]taste and season with more salt,pepper or vinegar.serve
